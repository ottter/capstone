Privacy and Upload Restrictions

Users are not just allowed to upload any piece of content they want. It is important that every feels safe when choosing to download files hosted by MGA Connect. To promise this to our users, we will have moderators that manage the uploads, in particular new users. Established users will be given a more hands-off approach once we know that they are a quality uploader.

Another restriction we take seriously is that users are not allowed to upload word-for-word copies of their instructors content, such as powerpoints, videos, or assignments.
We want our content to be 100% user-generated!
