Why we are here

This website was created by a group of Information Technology students with the goal of helping all MGA students. Here you can connect and collaborate with other students by sharing and studying notes taken by other students. Notes from all different courses and subjects can be easily uploaded and accessed here. Sign up with your MGA email and gain access to all of the features this site has to offer.
